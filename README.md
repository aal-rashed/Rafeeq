## What is Rafeeq?

**رفيق** is a chatbot that helps people in Saudi Arabia discover hobbies, clubs, and events that match their personality, lifestyle, and budget.

Instead of browsing endless lists, users have a natural Arabic conversation with Rafeeq. He asks questions, learns about their interests, and suggests real clubs from the Hawi platform — across all regions of Saudi Arabia.

---

## Features (so far)

- 🧭 **"اكتشف شغفي" Discovery Flow** — questions about interests, motivation, social style, time, and budget
- 🏛️ **Real Hawi Data** — 3,360+ clubs, 233 hobbies, 3,832 events across Saudi Arabia
- 📍 **Location-aware** — filters clubs by city and region
- 🌙 **Arabic-first** — full RTL design, natural Arabic conversation
- 📱 **Mobile-friendly** — built for phone screens
- ⚡ **No backend yet** — currently a single HTML file
